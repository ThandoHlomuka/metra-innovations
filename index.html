FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our software engineering and web development services in South Africa.

How much does a website cost in South Africa?

Website costs in South Africa vary based on complexity. A basic WordPress site starts from R5,000, while custom web applications and ecommerce platforms range from R25,000 to R100,000+. As leading web developers in South Africa, we provide transparent quotes tailored to your specific requirements. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote.

What should I look for when looking for website developers?

When looking for website developers, consider: portfolio quality, technical expertise (WordPress, ecommerce, custom development), client testimonials, POPIA compliance knowledge, local support availability, and pricing transparency. Our software engineers in South Africa offer all these plus free consultations to discuss your project.

Do you offer WordPress development services?

Yes! We are WordPress specialists in South Africa with extensive experience in custom WordPress development, theme customization, plugin development, and WooCommerce ecommerce solutions. Our WordPress developers create fast, secure, and SEO-optimized websites.

Are you ecommerce specialists who can build online stores?

Absolutely! As ecommerce specialists in South Africa, we build complete online stores with PayFast, Yoco, and Ozow payment integration, inventory management, courier integrations (Pudo, PAXI), and mobile-optimized shopping experiences. We've helped hundreds of South African businesses sell online successfully.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline depends on complexity: basic WordPress sites take 2-4 weeks, custom web applications 6-12 weeks, and large ecommerce platforms 8-16 weeks. Our web developer team in South Africa follows agile methodology with regular updates and demos throughout development.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive support packages including security updates, backups, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. As your dedicated developer in South Africa, we ensure your website remains secure, fast, and up-to-date.

Can you help with SEO and ranking my website?

Yes! All our websites are built with SEO best practices including fast loading speeds, mobile optimization, proper schema markup, and clean code. We also offer dedicated SEO services to help your website rank higher on Google for keywords relevant to your South African business.

Do you work with businesses outside Johannesburg?

Absolutely! While based in Johannesburg, we work with clients across South Africa including Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria, Port Elizabeth, Bloemfontein, and beyond. We offer remote consultations and support, making us accessible to businesses nationwide.
